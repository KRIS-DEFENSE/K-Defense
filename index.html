Empowering Weston Seniors Against Digital Fraud

Protecting Our Community

Knowledge Defense is an interactive simulator designed to help you spot "red flags" in scam messages. Inspired by real-world events in Weston, FL, our mission is to provide the tools needed to stay safe in a digital world.

Status: The simulator is currently being built. Interactive levels will launch on May 9th.
